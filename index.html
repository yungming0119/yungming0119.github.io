About me

I am a PhD student majoring geograghy in the University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

During my previous programs, I am engaged in several fields of study in GISciense, including space-time GIS, transportation geography, remote sensing, geographic data mining and computer programming. My masterâ€™s thesis is about space-time data mining for traffic network data. I developed program and algorithm for my thesis in C# computer language and also Python.
